Outcomes from Australia's National Study of Mental Health and Wellbeing 2020-2022

Introduction

From 2020-2022 the Australian Bureau of Statistics
conducted the National Study of Mental Health. This
visualisation will provide insights into the results of
this survey, highlighting the areas which require
extra resources to support Australians who suffer
from mental health conditions.

This survey covered anxiety, affective and substance use
disorders.This visualisation refers to lifetime and 12-month
mental disorders where lifetime mental disorders
refer to individuals who have met the diagnostic
criteria for a mental disorder at some point in
their life, but not necessarily throughout their
entire life. 12-month mental disorders refer to
those who met the criteria and also had symptoms in
the 12 months prior to the survey.

The dashboard will focus on 12-month and
lifetime mental disorders with symptoms
